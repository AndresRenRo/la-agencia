Questions, answered

What a fintech & crypto SEO agency actually does — and how we're different.

What is Beemio?

Beemio is a bilingual (English/Spanish), nearshore SEO agency for fintech and crypto brands. We help you rank on Google and get cited by AI engines like ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity.

What is AEO / GEO (AI visibility)?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) mean structuring your content and authority so AI assistants cite and recommend your brand when people ask them questions — the new complement to classic SEO.

Do you write in English and Spanish?

Yes — natively in both, not machine-translated. Ideal for fintech and crypto brands selling across the US, Europe and LATAM.

How are you different from a generalist SEO agency?

We specialize only in fintech and crypto, include AI visibility (AEO/GEO) in every engagement, work bilingually in your timezone, and report against revenue — all month-to-month.
