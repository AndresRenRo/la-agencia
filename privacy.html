Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Plain language, no surprises: this page explains what information Beemio collects, why, and what we do with it. Short version — we collect only what you send us, we use it to reply to you and do our work, and we never sell it.

Who we are

Beemio is a bilingual SEO and AI-visibility agency for fintech and crypto brands, operated from Mexico and Argentina. You can reach us anytime at hello@beemio.io.

What we collect

  • Audit request form: your name, work email, and website URL — because you typed them in and sent them to us.
  • Email: whatever you include when you write to us.
  • Basic technical logs: our hosting provider records standard server logs (like IP address and pages requested) to keep the site running and secure.

That's it. We don't run advertising trackers or sell data to third parties.

Why we use it

  • To reply to you and deliver the free audit you asked for.
  • To provide our services if we end up working together.
  • To keep the site secure and functioning.

We don't add you to a newsletter or drip campaign just because you requested an audit.

Who processes it for us

Like most small businesses, we rely on a few trusted service providers to operate:

  • Formspree — receives and forwards our contact-form submissions.
  • Vercel — hosts this website.
  • Zoho Mail — powers our email.

Each processes data only to provide their service to us, under their own privacy and security terms.

How long we keep it

We keep your inquiry for as long as it's useful to serve you — typically the duration of our conversation or engagement, plus a reasonable period afterward. If you'd like it gone sooner, just ask.

Your choices

Email hello@beemio.io and we'll do any of the following, promptly and without drama:

  • Tell you what information we hold about you.
  • Correct it.
  • Delete it.

Client audit pages

Audits we prepare for prospective clients live at private, unguessable links, are marked not to be indexed by search engines, and are intended only for the company they were prepared for.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the date at the top. Material changes will be noted clearly.

Questions?

Write to hello@beemio.io — a human reads it.
